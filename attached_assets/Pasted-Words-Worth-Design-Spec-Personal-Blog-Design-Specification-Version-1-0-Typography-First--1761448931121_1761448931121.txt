### Typography System

**Base Font Size:** 18px (larger than typical for enhanced readability)

**Font Families:**
- Primary: System font stack (default)
- Serif (for ornaments): Georgia, Times New Roman, serif
- Letter-spacing adjustments for visual refinement

**Type Scale:**
```
h1: 4-4.5rem (64-72px) | Line-height: 1.1 | Letter-spacing: -0.02em
h2: 3rem (48px) | Line-height: 1.3 | Letter-spacing: -0.01em  
h3: 1.875rem (30px) | Line-height: 1.4
Body: 1rem (18px) | Line-height: 1.75 (generous for reading)
Small: 0.75rem (12px) | Uppercase tracking: widest
```

**Font Weights:**
- Normal: 400 (body text)
- Medium: 500 (headings, labels)
- Light: 300 (special treatments)

**Special Typography Features:**
1. **Drop Caps** - First letter enlarged to 4.5rem, floated left
2. **Pull Quotes** - 2xl-3xl italic text with left border accent
3. **Ornamental Dividers** - Fleuron symbol "â¦" in Georgia serif

### Spacing & Layout

**Container Max-Widths:**
- Header: 80rem (1280px)
- Hero Section: 64rem (1024px)
- Blog Grid: 72rem (1152px)
- Article Content: 48rem (768px)
- About Page: 64rem (1024px)

**Padding Scale:**
```
px-4: 1rem (mobile)
px-6: 1.5rem (tablet+)
py-8: 2rem (sections)
py-12: 3rem (major sections)
py-16: 4rem (page sections)
py-20: 5rem (large sections)
```

**Grid System:**
- Blog Cards: 2-column grid on desktop (gap: 2rem)
- About Features: 3-column grid on desktop (gap: 3rem)
- Responsive: Collapses to single column on mobile

### Borders & Radius

**Border Widths:**
- Default: 1px
- Accent borders: 2-3px
- Left accent bars: 1-2px (grows on hover)

**Border Radius:**
- Cards: 0.5rem (subtle, refined)
- No border radius on images (editorial style)

### Shadows & Effects

**Hover Effects:**
- Card lift: translateY(-4px)
- Border color: foreground/20% opacity
- Smooth transitions: 300ms duration

**No drop shadows** - Uses borders instead for clean, editorial aesthetic

  id: number;

- Conversational, personal tone
- Paragraph breaks with \n\n
